Our Story

ShopWave was founded in 2019 with a simple belief — every Indian deserves access to quality products at fair prices, delivered right to their doorstep.

What started as a small electronics marketplace in Bengaluru has grown into one of India's fastest-growing multi-category ecommerce platforms, serving customers across 100+ cities.

Today, we partner with thousands of sellers, brands and manufacturers to bring you the widest selection of authentic products — from the latest smartphones to fresh groceries — all under one roof.
